ABOUT THE SOCIETY

The North West Golf Society is a friendly group of golfers with a range of experiance in the great game of Golf. We vary in experiance and strive and push each other to become better golfers through regualr matches and competitions. We are based in the North West of England and play regualry throughout the year. With one annual international match organised between us. Our Society currently has 46 members but the more the merrier!

Reasons to Join?

Practice Your Swing

Here at the North West Golf Society members are always looking to improve their swing. By playing regularly accross a host of different and challenging courses your ability will be tested. Furthermore, you will be playing alongside other members so can pick up on good habits and tips along the way.

Socialise

By joining this golf society, you can rest assured you're joining a great bunch of people who all have one thing in common, they love golf! This is an excellent opportunity to grow your social circle with like minded people and play alongside some talented golfers. This society is about more than just golf, its about making friends from all walks of life.
