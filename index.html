ABOUT THE SOCIETY

The North West Golf Society is a friendly group of golfers with a range of experiance in the great game of Golf. We vary in experiance and strive and push each other to become better golfers through regualr matches and competitions. We are based in the North West of England and play regualry throughout the year. With one annual international match organised between us. Our Society currently has 46 members but the more the merrier!
