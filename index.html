ABOUT THE SOCIETY

The North West Golf Society is a friendly group of golfers with a range of experiance in the great game of Golf. We vary in experiance and strive and push each other to become better golfers through regualr matches and competitions. We are based in the North West of England and play regualry throughout the year. With one annual international match organised between us. Our Society currently has 46 members but the more the merrier!

Reasons to Join?

Practice Your Swing

Here at the North West Golf Society members are always looking to improve their swing. By playing regularly accross a host of different courses your ability will be tested. Furthermore, you will be playing alongside other members.

Socialise

By joining this golf society, you're joining a great bunch of people who all love golf! This is an excellent opportunity to grow your social circle with like minded people. This society is about more than just golf.

The Challenge

There are some wonderful and difficult golf courses throughout the North West of England. Throughout Cumbria, Lancashire, Liverpool and Cheshire are many challenging courses in their own right.

International Event

There is the annual international event that is arranged to play in a more appealing sunny climate. Another opportunity to play at another excellent course. Previous trips include Portugal, Turkey and Dubai.
